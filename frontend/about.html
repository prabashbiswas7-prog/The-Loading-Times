Who We Are

The Loading Times is an independent news and satire publication that believes the best journalism is slow journalism. We're not chasing clicks — we're chasing truth, comedy, and the occasional factual error we'll correct three weeks later.

What We Cover

Everything. Nothing. Mostly the things that matter and the things that don't, presented with equal gravitas. From geopolitics to gossip, from technology to tea — if it's happening, we'll get around to reporting on it eventually.

Our Philosophy

In a world of breaking news and hot takes, The Loading Times proudly serves lukewarm analysis and carefully considered jokes. We believe every story deserves time, and every reader deserves honesty — even when the truth is funnier than fiction.
